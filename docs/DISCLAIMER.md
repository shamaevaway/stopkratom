# Medical and Safety Disclaimer

Tea Taper is not a medical device and is not a replacement for a doctor, therapist, addiction specialist, emergency service, or other qualified professional.

The app:

- Does not diagnose conditions
- Does not treat addiction or withdrawal
- Does not cure or prevent disease
- Does not prescribe dosing or tapering schedules
- Does not guarantee outcomes

If you experience severe withdrawal symptoms, suicidal thoughts, chest pain, fainting, seizures, severe dehydration, severe mental distress, or any urgent medical problem, contact local emergency services or a medical professional immediately.

For public release, add country-specific emergency resources and support links.
