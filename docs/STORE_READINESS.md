# Store readiness checklist

## Google Play

- Complete Health apps declaration if classified as health-related.
- Provide privacy policy URL.
- Provide support contact.
- Avoid claims that app treats, cures, diagnoses, or prevents disease.
- Avoid drug-promotion language.
- Complete Data Safety form accurately.
- Test Android app bundle with internal testing.
- Add account deletion path if accounts are supported.
- Add screenshots and store listing copy focused on journaling and reduction tracking.

## App Store

- Provide privacy policy URL.
- Complete App Privacy details accurately.
- Avoid medical treatment claims.
- Make all links functional.
- Make account deletion available if account creation is available.
- Add support URL.
- Test on real iPhone.
- If Apple asks, state app is not a regulated medical device and does not provide treatment.

## Thailand-specific positioning

- No sale of kratom.
- No affiliate links to kratom sellers.
- No mixing, sourcing, or effect-enhancement instructions.
- 18+ gate.
- Reduction journal positioning only.
