# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-19

Tea Taper is a private intake and wellbeing tracker. It is not a medical service.

## Data you may enter

- Kratom tea volume in milliliters
- Dates and times of entries
- Daily notes
- Wellbeing scores from 0 to 10
- Triggers and helper tags
- App settings
- Account email if cloud sync is enabled

## Local storage

By default, data is stored on your device using browser/app storage.

## Cloud storage

If you sign in, your data can be synchronized to Firebase Cloud Firestore under your authenticated user ID.

## Data sharing

We do not sell user data. The app does not contain third-party advertising SDKs in this source package.

## Health data

Your entries may be sensitive. Do not enter information you do not want stored.

## Export and deletion

You can export JSON/CSV from the app. You can delete local data inside the app. If cloud sync is enabled, use the cloud delete action or contact support.

## Medical disclaimer

Tea Taper does not diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent disease and does not replace professional medical care.
